# bme-knowledge-base
Game AI is generally different from popular Generative AI technologies it is different algorithms to make games more realistic though some games using Machine learning to constantly upgrade their enemies and NPC's.
It modifies, intelligence of enemies, makes the difficulty adjustable, and helps them to find their way.
Tools/frameworks: Unity ML-Agents, Unreal Behavior Trees, TensorFlow. 
Topics: pathfinding (A*), NPC behaviors.
